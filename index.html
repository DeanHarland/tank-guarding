Tank Guarding

Protect the team. Control the fight. Lead from the front.

Learn how the Tank role keeps teammates safe, controls danger, and creates a secure environment for success in team-based games.

Maintaining Aggro

  • Keep enemy attention on yourself at all times.
  • Use threat-generating abilities regularly.
  • Position enemies to protect your teammates.

Using Defensives

  • Use cooldowns to mitigate incoming damage.
  • Monitor your health and defensive resources.
  • Coordinate with teammates to survive high-damage phases.
